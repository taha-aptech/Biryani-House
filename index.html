About Karachi Biryani House

Founded in 1985, Karachi Biryani House has been serving the most authentic and flavorful biryani in Karachi for over three decades. Our secret family recipes and traditional cooking methods ensure that every plate of biryani delivers an unforgettable culinary experience.

We source the finest ingredients, including premium basmati rice, fresh meat, and a carefully balanced blend of spices that create the signature taste of our biryani. Our commitment to quality and authenticity has made us a favorite destination for biryani lovers across Pakistan.

Whether you're craving our famous chicken biryani, succulent mutton biryani, or flavorful aloo biryani, we guarantee a meal that will transport your taste buds to the heart of Pakistani cuisine.
